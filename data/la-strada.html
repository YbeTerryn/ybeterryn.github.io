Imagine writing a character that's not only incompetent and mentally challenged but also considered ugly (looking like a very unflattering vegetable) and asking your wife to play it. The mere size of Fellini's balls keeps surprising me. And what a joy she is in the movie! Giulietta Masina simply shines. And, for me, she is actually the only thing that elevates this film to a higher rating. Because story- and cinematography wise this film is far from Fellini's most interesting.
