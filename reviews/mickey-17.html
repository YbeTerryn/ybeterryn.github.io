Instead of tackling the impossible task of trying to top Parasite, with Mickey 17 Bong Joon-ho seems to be mostly enjoying himself. That doesn't mean however that this sci-fi adventure is without it's social commentary. The pretty straightforward (and maybe even predictable) narrative that explores the premise of being an expandable in an imperialistic space program, has it's moment of critique, of colonizing species, of politicians and of scientific experiments. It's just much more reminiscent of other films in it's genre, even straight blockbusters like The Hungers games or Divergent. Don't expect to much originality from the plot. The way it's delivered doesn't help either. It's worst feature is the over-used voice narration. Bong Joon-ho does however entertains in presentation and cinematography and is what sets him apart form those previously mentioned. Robert Pattinson's acting even gets a few laughs out of it's audience. The Creepers also kind of reminded me a bit of Okja for some reason.
