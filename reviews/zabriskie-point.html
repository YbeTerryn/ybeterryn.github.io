Zabriskie Point

Zabriskie point is a dream, a statement, a fantasy of freedom. It is about nothing and everything at the same time. Even in it's surrealistic presentation, Michelangelo Antonioni serves his audience a clear message and an easy to follow metaphor.
Going from harsh reality into unbridled love and back, Zabriskie Point is powerful, sad and hopeful at the same time. Definitely a product of it's time, but one to cherish for many generations to come.
