Man, The Misfortunates might be the most unfortunate translation of a title I've yet seen. 'De Helaasheid Der Dingen' is a film that is able to go from absurdist comedy to emotionally heavy so quickly and almost without effort. The brothers are phenomenally trashy in this evocation of Dimitri Verhulst's tragic upbringing. Everyone who has seen Belgian marginality, knows this is much closer to reality, than is comfortable. Some of the adult Gunther Strobbe scene's bring the film down, but it is one to explore nonetheless.
