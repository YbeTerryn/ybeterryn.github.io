The compositions are beautiful, the presentation is poetic, but that's really where it ends for me. I nodded at every scene, going, this looks indeed very gorgeous, but nothing more. No real emotions or evocations. No real feelings. In contrast with other deeply poetic films like Zerkalo or Koyaanisqatsi, Sayat Nova pales. Still, sergei Parajanov's project deserves applaus for it's marvelous attention to detail, even if it is a bit of a chore to finish.
