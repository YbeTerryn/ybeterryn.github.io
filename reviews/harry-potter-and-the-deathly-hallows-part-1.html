This is by far the worst entry in the Franchise. It's a confusing mess of scenes with very little engagement. I've had my fair share of squabbles with the Harry Potter series, but so far they have all been watchable and might I add, even enjoyable. The Deathly Hallows: Part 1 is the first time I was looking forward to the end. So many random characters are introduced and then just vanish. The film feels like a crash course of someone explaining the plot of the books. Also bringing back a character we cared for six movies ago just kill him off for shock value is such a season 8 Game of Thrones move. Garbage. I might as well agree with Voldemort and become a death eater at this point, since the horcrux scene of Harry and Hermione kissing is the most chemistry any couple has ever had. Two star to be given. One is for the story of the three brothers animation sequence and the other one is for Nick Cave.
