Caught stealing biggest (and almost only) feature is it's plot. It's subtle twist after another, but the logical flow is never lost. While I applaud a director going for a totally different style and was very excited that Darren Aronofsky was the one doing it, the action comedy realm seems not suited for him. There are occasional genius shots but the overall tone of the film feels off. It ain't that funny, and it ain't really that action packed. The balance between drama, action and comedy just isn't there. Austin Butler looks cool though. Ps: Hiding Laura Dern is a bold movie, but one I appreciated.
