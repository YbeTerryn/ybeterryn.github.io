The Texas Chain Saw Massacre

The whole movie, I felt like I was not in on the joke. This thing as one of the greatest horror films of al time? Wasn't even scary, just a bit distubring.
The influence of this film cannot be understated. But maybe it shouldn't have been that influential? There's not a single slasher genre trope that this film produced, that I actually like. The final girl, the mask, the stupid way in which everyone always splits up...
My stepbrother, who was watching with me, summed up the movie pretty well. A half hour of driving, a half hour of poeple going stupidly into the house of a murderer and than a full half hour of screeching, earraping screams.
