From the first scene, the cinematography is bold and while entertaining, it isn't till the ending that it reaches it's full potential. The comedy is slow and subtle, but enjoyable. And then in an ending ten minute take it throws all the comedy out the window and lets the mean themes collision head-on. Truly captivating and moving cinema, like I haven't seen in 2025 thus far. There is no explaining how good Jafar Panahi ends his film. A statement of a film that has only a few less interesting moments.
