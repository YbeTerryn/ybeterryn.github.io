Nightshift, while slow is packed with humor. And the 'jokes' if you can call them that, are hidden between the shots rather than in them, which is a very unique feeling. Not as hypnotizing as Chantal Akermans work which it is often compared to, but very enjoyable nonetheless. The only thing bothering me is the ending, since I found the make up removal and slowly walking out the doorframe such a fitting and poetic ending, but then the film kept going for another ten minutes for some reason.
