While filled with strong moments all around, Adolescence main feature, the one take episodes, make up for a lot of questionable choices in it's narrative. It's impressionistic, mosaic depiction of teen violence and culture tries to encompass more themes than it really can hold. It's solid, don't get me wrong, the four stars are well deserved. But when the hype dies down, I truly question if it will still be regarded as one of the strongest pieces of television, since it has some obvious flaws.
